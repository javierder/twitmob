What is MiCrowder.com...?


Step 1: Create your Crowd: Here.
There you'll be able to create your crowd by selecting your #hashtag and choose a time and a date to schedule your twitter bomb.

Step 2: After creating your Crowd, you'll be able to add messages/tweets for it.
You can add as much as you want; and you can even allow your crowders to add their own.

Step 3: Then...don't forget to choose the one you'll send yourself!

Step 4: Make sure you promote your Crowd, via twitter or any way you want!
You'll want as much crowders as possible in your campaign!

They'll be able to select the message they want to tweet with your hashtag.

Step 5: When the scheduled time for your Crowd starts...we'll start tweeting the messages you and your crowders selected.
We'll tweet all the messages we can for 1 hour since your Crowd starts!

Well...that's all!
Remember this is a very alpha release! Send any comments to @microwdercom!
